Features

Everything a PM needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built around real PM workflows — not a generic document editor with AI sprinkled on top.

✦
AI-Native Document Editor

A beautiful, distraction-free writing environment where AI is a first-class citizen. Press ⌘K anywhere to invoke AI — generate a PRD, break an epic into tickets, draft a stakeholder update, or synthesize research — all inline, all in context. The AI sees your entire document and product context before it writes a single word.

🧠
Product Brain

Paste your product strategy, target users, and constraints once. Every AI output — across every document — is automatically grounded in it. No more re-explaining your product every time.

💬
Contextual AI Chat

Chat with your document. Ask it to challenge your assumptions, identify gaps in your PRD, suggest edge cases you missed, or simplify a section for executives. It reads your doc so you don't have to paste it.

🎯
AI Apply — Cursor-Style Diffs

When AI suggests improvements, see exactly what it wants to change — highlighted inline like code diffs. Accept all, reject all, or review change by change. No more copy-pasting from a chat window.

🖼️
Multimodal UI Review

Attach a screenshot of your product and ask AI to review it from a PM perspective — UX gaps, missing states, copy inconsistencies. Turn design reviews into actionable feedback in seconds.

📂
Knowledge Base with RAG

Upload your research reports, competitive analyses, user interview transcripts, and past PRDs. AI answers are automatically enriched with relevant excerpts — cited, not hallucinated.

🎫
Ticket Generator

Turn a PRD into a full epic breakdown — stories with acceptance criteria, story points, and implementation details — ready to export directly to Jira or Linear with one click.

📋
Template Library

Start from battle-tested PM templates: PRD, OKR planning, interview synthesis, sprint review, competitor analysis, one-pagers. Each one AI-fillable from a single prompt.

🔍
Global Search

Find anything across all your documents, research, and knowledge base — powered by semantic search. Ask a question, get the relevant context. Not just keyword matching.

🔗
Linear & Jira Integration Soon

Push tickets directly from PMind into your existing project management workflow. No copy-paste, no reformatting — just sync.

👥
Team Workspaces Soon

A shared Product Brain, collaborative documents, and team-wide knowledge base. Every PM on your team working from the same product context.
