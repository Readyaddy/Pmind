chat about how the reader works** — free product access is the thank-you,
not the pitch.

## Notes before posting

- Replace **[link]** with wherever the video lives (Loom is good — gives you
  per-viewer engagement signals you can mention in the chat).
- Read the channel rules. Most PM Slacks (Lenny's, Mind the Product) require
  self-promo to live in `#showcase` / `#share-your-work` only.
- Best time: **Tue or Wed, 9–11am** in the community's primary timezone.
- The "no pitch, just listening" framing is load-bearing — only use it if you
  can keep your hands off the demo button during the call. Otherwise it
  reads as bait.
- After the call, send them the access link **within an hour** while the
  conversation is still warm.
