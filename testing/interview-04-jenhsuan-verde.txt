USER INTERVIEW — Jen-Hsuan W., COO, "Verde Skincare"
Company: Small skincare brand, ~600 orders/mo
Product: Shopflow
Interviewer: Maya R. (Product)
Date: Jan 18, 2026

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Maya: First impression of Shopflow when you signed up?

Jen-Hsuan: Honestly, friendly. The marketing site is great. The trial gave
me 14 days, which felt fair. But the moment I tried to actually connect
to Shopify, the OAuth flow timed out twice. I almost gave up. I only got
through because I'd seen a Reddit thread saying you have to use Chrome
incognito — Safari just hangs forever.

Maya: That's a known thing?

Jen-Hsuan: Apparently. Why is it not on your help page? I spent an hour on
this. For a smaller brand evaluating five tools that week, that's enough
to lose the deal.

Maya: Beyond setup?

Jen-Hsuan: The interface is fine. The thing that hurts is — we're tiny. We
don't need most of what Growth tier offers, but Starter cuts us off from
multi-warehouse, and we have a fulfilment partner in Texas plus our
garage in California. So we're forced to Growth at $399 just for that
one feature. I'd happily pay $150 for "small business with 2 warehouses"
but that doesn't exist.

Maya: How are you feeling about renewing?

Jen-Hsuan: 60/40 yes. The forecasting and the Shopify sync (once it
works) are solid. But if a competitor launches a more honest pricing
tier in the next 90 days, I'm gone.
