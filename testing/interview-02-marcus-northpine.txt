USER INTERVIEW — Marcus T., Founder, "Northpine Coffee Co."
Company: DTC coffee, ~8k orders/mo
Product: Shopflow
Interviewer: Maya R. (Product)
Date: Jan 12, 2026

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Maya: You've been on Shopflow for, what, 6 months now?

Marcus: Five. And I almost cancelled in month two. The onboarding nearly
killed me. I had to manually map every SKU from Shopify, Faire, AND our
wholesale platform. No CSV import wizard, no auto-match. I spent a
weekend on it. My wife was furious.

Maya: Did you talk to your CSM?

Marcus: Our CSM is good — Priya — but she said the onboarding tool just
isn't built for multi-channel sellers yet. So she does it manually for
big accounts. Why isn't there a wizard? Every competitor has one.

Maya: What about pricing — how's that landed?

Marcus: The pricing page is genuinely confusing. There's a Starter tier at
$99 but it caps you at 1000 orders and we hit that in week one. So we
went to "Growth" at $399 which includes "premium support" — except
premium support is just email with a 24h SLA. The seat-based add-ons
aren't explained anywhere. I budgeted $400/mo and we're paying $680.
That's a trust issue.

Maya: If you could change one thing tomorrow?

Marcus: Show me the total cost on the pricing page before I click. Just
build a calculator. Stop hiding the seat fee under a footnote.
