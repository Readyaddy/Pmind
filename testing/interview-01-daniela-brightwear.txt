USER INTERVIEW — Daniela K., Operations Lead, "Brightwear"
Company: Apparel brand, ~40k SKUs
Product: Shopflow
Interviewer: Maya R. (Product)
Date: Jan 8, 2026

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Maya: Walk me through a typical Monday morning.

Daniela: Honestly? I open Shopflow and immediately dread the reconciliation
screen. We process maybe 800 orders over the weekend and the dashboard
takes — I'm not exaggerating — close to a minute to load. My team starts
the day standing around waiting for the page to render. I've timed it.

Maya: What do you do while you wait?

Daniela: Coffee, mostly. But it's not just slow — when it does load, the
filters reset every single time. Every morning I have to re-apply
"unfulfilled" + "marketplace = Amazon" + "ship-by today". That's three
clicks I make 250 times a year. It's the kind of thing that makes you
hate software.

Maya: Have you mentioned this to support?

Daniela: Twice. They told me it's a known issue but there's "no ETA". That
phrase is poison. Just tell me it's not coming. We're now seriously
evaluating Linnworks because at least their filters stick.

Maya: What else?

Daniela: Bulk editing. If I want to change supplier on 200 SKUs I have to do
it one at a time. There's no select-all-and-edit. I built a Google Sheet
workaround that uses the API but our junior staff can't run it, so it's
just me at 9pm on Sundays.

Maya: That sounds painful.

Daniela: It's a churn risk for us, I'll be honest. If we hit Q2 and this
isn't better I have to defend the cost to my CEO.
