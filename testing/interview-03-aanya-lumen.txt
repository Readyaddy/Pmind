USER INTERVIEW — Aanya P., Inventory Manager, "Lumen Lighting"
Company: Lighting wholesaler, ~120k SKUs
Product: Shopflow
Interviewer: Maya R. (Product)
Date: Jan 15, 2026

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Maya: How does your team use Shopflow?

Aanya: Mostly through the app on the warehouse floor. And that's where the
mobile experience just falls apart. The barcode scanner integration drops
the connection if there's a single weak wifi spot — and our warehouse has
about six of those. Every time it drops, the scan history is gone. The
scanners on Cin7 hold onto a local queue. Yours doesn't.

Maya: Have your warehouse staff complained?

Aanya: They've stopped complaining. They just go back to clipboards. Which
defeats the entire point of paying you. Last quarter we re-counted 6,000
units because of phantom inventory caused by missed scans.

Maya: That's... rough. Six thousand?

Aanya: Six thousand. I have the audit report. Our finance team treats it
as a known tax on using this software now.

Maya: What about your QuickBooks sync?

Aanya: Breaks weekly. We file a ticket every Monday morning. Same error
every time — "credential refresh required". Nobody's fixed the root
cause; we just reconnect. It's been 9 months.

Maya: Anything you genuinely like?

Aanya: The forecasting model is actually really good when it works. The
demand prediction for Q4 was within 3%. So I know your team CAN ship
quality. That's what's so frustrating.
